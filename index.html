Sunflower

The sunflower seed is the fruit of the sunflower. There are three types of commonly used sunflower seeds: linoleic, high oleic, and sunflower oil seeds.

Daisy

Bellis perennis, the daisy, is a European species of the family Asteraceae, often considered the archetypal species of the name daisy.one of the most popular perennial flowers.

Chrysanths

Chrysanthemums, sometimes called mums or chrysanths, are flowering plants of the genus Chrysanthemum in the family Asteraceae. They are native to East Asia and northeastern Europe.
